🛍 UB City Mall

📜 Overview

UB City Mall is Bengaluru’s premier luxury destination, developed by the UB Group in collaboration with Prestige Group. Often referred to as the “Rodeo Drive of Bangalore”, it brings together high-end retail, fine dining, nightlife, and business — all in one sophisticated space.

✨ Highlights

  • Home to international luxury brands like Louis Vuitton, Rolex, Burberry, Jimmy Choo, and more.
  • Skyye Lounge: Rooftop bar with panoramic views of Bengaluru’s skyline.
  • Sublime Galleria: India’s first sky art gallery, hosting exhibitions, music, and fashion events.
  • Global fine dining options: Italian, Japanese, Mediterranean, and fusion cuisines.
  • Architectural elegance with open-air plazas and modern cascading fountains.
