⛪ St. Mary’s Basilica

📜 Overview

St. Mary’s Basilica is the oldest church in Bengaluru and was the first in Karnataka to be designated as a Minor Basilica in 1973. Dedicated to Mother Mary, it showcases stunning Gothic-style architecture and holds deep spiritual significance for the Christian community in the region. Originally built in the 17th century, the current structure was completed in 1875.

✨ Highlights

  • Elegant stained glass windows, tall pointed arches, and a 160-ft tall spire.
  • The statue of Mother Mary holding Baby Jesus is paraded during the grand St. Mary’s Feast in September.
  • The feast attracts thousands across faiths, making it one of Bengaluru’s largest religious celebrations.
  • Interior features include ornate altars and life-sized saint statues.
