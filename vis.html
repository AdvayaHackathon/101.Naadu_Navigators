🏛 Overview The Visvesvaraya Industrial & Technological Museum (VITM) was established in 1962 as a tribute to Sir M. Visvesvaraya, a visionary engineer and statesman. It is operated by the National Council of Science Museums (NCSM), Ministry of Culture, Government of India. Aimed at promoting scientific learning and technological awareness, the museum attracts students, families, and tourists alike. ✨ Highlights The museum features seven permanent exhibition galleries, including: Engine Hall – real industrial machines and engines Fun Science – interactive science experiments and illusions Electro Technic – basics of electricity and electronics Space – Emerging Technology in the Service of Mankind – space exploration exhibits Biotechnological Revolution – latest in biotech discoveries Science for Kids – hands-on science play for children BEL Hall of Electronics – electronic gadgets and systems Popular attractions: Science Demonstration Shows 3D shows Taramandal (mini planetarium) Artificial Dinosaur model Live science experiments and activities 🧭 Visitor Information Perfect for families, school groups, students, and science enthusiasts. Highly interactive and child-friendly; many exhibits are touch-and-try. Guided tours and demonstration shows run throughout the day. Suitable for ages 5 and up; ideal for educational outings. 🕒 Timings Open all days of the week (except major public holidays like Deepavali and Republic Day). 🕘 9:30 AM to 6:00 PM 💰 Entry Fee General Admission: Adults: ₹85 Children (up to 5 years): Free Students in groups (organized): ₹40 (with letter from school) Extra Charges: 3D show: ₹30 Taramandal show: ₹10 Science show: ₹10 Kids Gallery (additional): ₹10 Prices may vary slightly; discounts are available for educational institutions. 📍 Location Address: Kasturba Road, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560001, India Located opposite to Cubbon Park and near UB City Mall, in the heart of the city. Nearest Metro: Cubbon Park Station or M.G. Road Station (Purple Line) Easily accessible by bus, auto-rickshaw, and cab.
🏞 Facilities Cafeteria offering snacks and beverages. Souvenir shop with science kits, toys, and books. Clean restrooms, drinking water, and seating areas. Wheelchair access and assistance for specially-abled visitors. Parking space available nearby (limited during weekends). Guided tours and educational workshops on request.
