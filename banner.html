🦁 Bannerghatta Biological Park

🌿 Overview

Bannerghatta Biological Park (BBP) is a unique reserve on the outskirts of Bengaluru that includes a zoo, safari park, butterfly park, and rescue center. It's ideal for wildlife lovers, students, and families seeking a day close to nature.

🌟 Highlights

  • Grand Safari: Ride through Lion, Tiger, Bear, and Herbivore zones via jeep or bus.
  • Zoo: 1,500+ animals including leopards, elephants, crocodiles, and exotic birds.
  • Butterfly Park: India's first, with a conservatory, museum, and AV room.
  • Rescue Center: Shelter for rescued animals (especially big cats).
  • Boating & Nature Trails: Available on the park’s periphery.

📅 Visitor Information

🕐 Timings:
Open: 9:30 AM to 5:00 PM
Weekly Holiday: Tuesdays Closed

⏳ Suggested Duration: 3 to 5 hours
📸 Camera Fee: Extra charges for cameras
🚌 Safari Timings: 10:00 AM – 4:30 PM (Last entry varies)

💵 Entry Fees (Approximate)

  • Safari (Indian Citizens): ₹300–₹400 (Adults), ₹150–₹250 (Children 6–12)
  • Students: Discounts available with ID
  • Butterfly Park: ₹30–₹50 (or included in some packages)
  • Zoo Only: ₹80–₹100 (Adults), ₹40–₹50 (Children)

🎟 Online Booking Available

📍 Location & Map

Bannerghatta Road, Bengaluru, Karnataka – 560083
About 22 km south of Bangalore city center, accessible by road.

🚇 Nearest Metro Station

Jayaprakash Nagar (Green Line) — ~13–15 km from the park. Use BMTC bus, auto, or cab to reach BBP.

🌤 Best Time to Visit

  • Season: October to March (cooler, better wildlife viewing)
  • Avoid: Monsoon (June–Sep) & hot summer afternoons
  • Time of Day: Arrive by 10:00 AM for best safari experience
