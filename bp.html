Bangalore Palace

A Royal Retreat in the Heart of Bengaluru

🏰 Overview

Built in 1878 by Chamarajendra Wadiyar, Bangalore Palace is a magnificent example of Tudor and Scottish Gothic architecture. Inspired by Windsor Castle in England, the palace reflects regal splendor through its wooden interiors, fortified towers, and lush gardens.

🌟 Highlights
  • Ornate wooden carvings, floral motifs, and stained-glass windows
  • Durbar Hall – a royal chamber with rich decor
  • Portrait gallery and memorabilia of the Wadiyar dynasty
  • Sprawling lawns for exhibitions and cultural events
  • Tudor-style battlements and turrets
🧭 Visitor Information
  • Ideal for: History lovers, architecture enthusiasts, and photographers
  • Average Duration: 1.5 to 2 hours
  • Audio Guide: Available in multiple languages
  • Photography: Allowed (with extra charges)
